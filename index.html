03 — Projects

Selected work.

A mix of thesis research, coursework and applied collaborations — spanning deep learning, radar, and 3D point clouds.

Thesis · Deep Learning

Forest Disturbance Driver Classification

A year-long end-to-end project: processing raw forestry records, researching disturbance domains, and training a ResUNet model to classify forest disturbance drivers across EU forests using Sentinel-1/2 time series.

Sentinel-1/2 ResUNet PyTorch Python

Integration Course · Sweco & Gemeente Amsterdam

Subsurface Root Detection with GPR

Explored ground-penetrating radar to detect tree roots beneath Amsterdam streets, using point cloud clustering and root object reconstruction.

GPR Point Clouds CloudCompare

Independent Analysis · NDWI

Flood Extent Mapping, Limburg 2021

Used NDWI derived from satellite imagery to compare normal water extent near Maastricht and Valkenburg (August 2024) against the July 2021 Limburg floods, visualizing how far the water spread beyond the riverbanks.

NDWI Copernicus Python

Advanced Remote Sensing · Groningen & Wageningen

3D Urban LiDAR Analysis

Built an interactive 3D map of building heights across Wageningen from DEM/DSM data, and processed AHN LiDAR scans of Zernike Campus to assess change in urban tree greenery over time.

LiDAR (AHN) 3D Visualization Python

Data Science Minor · Groningen

Energy Sector Customer Analysis

Analyzed a French industrial building database and regional energy-label data for a client in the renewable energy sector, mapping where poorly-insulated large buildings are concentrated to identify potential customers.

Python R Data Analysis

Geoinformation Tools · with David Restrepo Berrío

Iberian Ibex Movement Corridor

Modeled a habitat suitability corridor and likely movement path for the Iberian Ibex in Southern Spain, combining terrain and land-cover suitability layers to support wildlife corridor planning.

Suitability Modeling GIS

Geoinformation Tools · Wageningen

Connected Wageningen: Light Rail Suitability

Proposed a light rail route and stop locations for Wageningen, modeling walking-distance catchment areas to show that more than 70% of the city would fall within walking distance of a station.

Network Analysis Suitability Mapping

Personal Project · Home Region

Urban Sprawl Timelapse, Bratislava

A satellite imagery timelapse tracking urban expansion on the edges of Bratislava, Slovakia between 2006 and 2025 — a side project inspired by watching my own hometown grow.

Landsat Time Series
